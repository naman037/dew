Our Story

Crafted with Purpose

Founded in 2015, LUXE began as a small, intimate studio in the heart of Milan with a single, uncompromising vision: to redefine modern elegance through the lens of traditional Italian craftsmanship.

Our journey started with a group of artisans who believed that true luxury shouldn't just be seen—it should be felt. Every stitch we place tells a story of dedication, and every garment we create is a testament to the hands that shaped it. We don't just design clothes; we preserve a legacy.

The Design Process

From the selection of the finest Italian wools to the final fitting, our process is an intimate dialogue between the designer and the raw material.

We source only the highest grade natural fibers—silk from Como, cashmere from the Biella region, and wool that feels like a second skin. Each season begins with a sensory exploration of texture and weight, ensuring our materials meet the LUXE standard of excellence.
